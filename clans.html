How FWA Clan Points Work

FWA Clan Points are an internal performance metric used within the Farm War Alliance (FWA) ecosystem.

  • War stars & results – Consistent high-star wars earn more points
  • Rule compliance – Correct mirror attacks and zero violations
  • Activity consistency – No missed attacks across wars
  • Penalties – Missed attacks or rule breaks reduce points

These points determine a clan’s league placement (Champions, Masters, etc.) and are not related to in-game trophies or CWL medals.
