StatHut Clan Rules

To keep Farm War Alliance (FWA) wars fair and beneficial, all members are expected to follow these rules.

War Participation

  • Use both war attacks in every war
  • Attack only assigned targets
  • Follow timing instructions
  • Inform leaders if unavailable

Base Layouts

  • Use approved FWA base layouts
  • Do not modify bases during war
  • Keep defensive traps as instructed

Loot & Farming Etiquette

  • Focus on loot, not stars
  • No freestyle or unnecessary attacks
  • Respect farming agreements

Account Standards

  • Avoid excessive rushing
  • Follow clan-specific Town Hall rules
  • Heroes and spells must be available when required

Behavior & Conduct

  • Be respectful to members and staff
  • Follow Discord guidelines
  • No toxic or abusive behavior

Rule Enforcement

Failure to follow rules may result in warnings, demotion, or removal from the clan to protect the FWA system.
