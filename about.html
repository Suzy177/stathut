About Farm War Alliance (FWA)

Stress-free wars focused on loot, XP, and upgrades.

What is FWA?

Farm War Alliance (FWA) is a coordinated war system where clans intentionally plan wars to maximize resources instead of competition.

How It Works

Clans rotate wins, bases follow agreed layouts, and players complete attacks for guaranteed loot and experience.

Why Choose FWA?

Faster upgrades, relaxed gameplay, and no pressure to triple. Ideal for hero upgrades and casual farming.
