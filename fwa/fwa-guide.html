How Farm War Alliance (FWA) Works

This guide explains what Farm War Alliance (FWA) is, how FWA wars work, and how StatHut helps players farm efficiently without pressure.

What is Farm War Alliance (FWA)?

Farm War Alliance (FWA) is a war system where clans coordinate wars to maximize loot and upgrades instead of competitive results. Wars are planned, attacks are controlled, and both clans benefit from predictable outcomes.

Unlike normal wars, FWA focuses on resource farming, hero upgrades, and stress-free progression.

How Do FWA Wars Work?

  • FWA clans are matched against other FWA clans
  • Both sides agree on base layouts and attack rules
  • One clan wins, the other loses
  • Results rotate in future wars to keep things fair
Every player is expected to complete their attacks and follow instructions to keep the system working smoothly.

Why Do FWA Bases Look Strange?

FWA bases are intentionally designed to allow easy attacks. This ensures:

  • All players can complete attacks successfully
  • Maximum loot is gained
  • No time is wasted on failed attempts

These bases are approved and must not be modified during war.

What Rules Do Players Follow?

  • Attack only assigned targets
  • Use approved FWA base layouts
  • Complete both war attacks
  • No freestyle or trophy-focused attacks
  • Follow leadership instructions

Full details are available on the Rules page.

Is FWA Safe?

Yes. FWA has existed for many years and operates through structured, community-managed coordination. When rules are followed, players benefit from consistent loot and smooth progression.
