StatHut Clan Rules

To keep Farm War Alliance (FWA) wars fair, organized, and stress-free, all members are expected to follow these rules.

War Participation

  • Use both war attacks every war
  • Attack only assigned targets
  • Follow timing instructions from leadership
  • Inform leaders if you are unavailable

Base Layouts

  • Use approved FWA base layouts only
  • Do not modify bases during war
  • Keep defensive traps as instructed

Loot & Farming Etiquette

  • Focus on loot, not stars
  • No freestyle attacks in war
  • Respect farming agreements

Account Standards

  • Avoid extreme rushing
  • Maintain reasonable hero levels for your Town Hall
  • Always follow clan and FWA instructions
