How Farm War Alliance (FWA) Works

A beginner-friendly guide to understanding FWA wars in Clash of Clans and how StatHut helps players farm smarter.

What is FWA?

Farm War Alliance (FWA) is a system where clans coordinate wars to maximize loot and upgrades instead of competitive war performance. Wins are planned, attacks are controlled, and players benefit from guaranteed resources.

How Do FWA Wars Work?

FWA clans are matched against other FWA clans. Both sides agree on base layouts and attack rules. One clan wins, the other loses, and the result alternates in future wars to keep things fair.

Why Do FWA Bases Look Strange?

Bases are intentionally designed to allow easy attacks. This ensures everyone completes their attacks and gains maximum loot without unnecessary stress or failed attempts.

What Are the Rules?

• Follow assigned targets
• Use approved base layouts
• Complete all war attacks
• No freestyle or trophy pushing in war
• Respect clan coordination

Is FWA Safe?

Yes. FWA operates within Clash of Clans mechanics and has been used safely by organized clans for years when rules are followed correctly.

Why Join an FWA Clan?

FWA is ideal for players upgrading heroes, walls, and defenses. You get consistent loot, predictable wars, and a relaxed environment focused on long-term progression.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is FWA allowed in Clash of Clans?

Yes. Farm War Alliance (FWA) operates within normal Clash of Clans mechanics and has been used by thousands of clans for years.

Can rushed accounts join FWA?

It depends on the clan. Some StatHut clans accept slightly rushed bases, while others require balanced progression.

Do I need to attack in every war?

Yes. Completing assigned war attacks is mandatory to keep FWA wars fair and beneficial for everyone.

How long does clan placement take?

Placement is usually quick after joining Discord and sharing your Town Hall level and player tag.
