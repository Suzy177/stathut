How Farm War Alliance (FWA) Works

A beginner-friendly guide to understanding FWA in Clash of Clans and how StatHut helps players farm smarter.

What is FWA?

Farm War Alliance (FWA) is a coordinated war system where clans intentionally plan wars to maximize loot and upgrades instead of competitive performance.

How Do FWA Wars Work?

Clans matched against each other agree on base layouts and attack rules. One clan wins, the other loses, and the result alternates in future wars to keep things fair.

Why Choose FWA?

FWA wars allow players to safely complete attacks, gain maximum loot, and upgrade heroes without pressure or stress.

What Are the Rules?

  • Follow assigned targets
  • Use approved base layouts
  • Complete all war attacks
  • No unnecessary triple pushing
  • Respect coordination rules

Is FWA Safe?

Yes. FWA operates within Clash of Clans mechanics and has been used safely by organized clans for years when rules are followed.

Why Join an FWA Clan?

FWA is ideal for players upgrading heroes, walls, and defenses who want consistent loot and a relaxed environment focused on long-term progression.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is FWA allowed in Clash of Clans?

Yes. FWA works within normal Clash of Clans war mechanics and has been used by thousands of clans.

Can rushed accounts join?

Some clans accept rushed bases for farming, while others require balanced progression.

Do I need to attack in every war?

Yes. Completing assigned attacks is mandatory to keep wars fair and beneficial for everyone.

How long does clan placement take?

Placement is usually quick after joining Discord and sharing your Town Hall level and player tag.
